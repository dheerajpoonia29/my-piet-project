Kim Taeyoung


Kim Taeyong, a South Korean rapper, singer, dancer, and songwriter. He is best known as a member and leader of the boy group NCT, and its sub-units NCT U and NCT 127.
He also debuted as part of the supergroup SuperM. In addition to his group activities, Taeyong has released solo music, including the single "Long Flight".
He is also recognized for his songwriting skills, having contributed to over 30 songs across various languages, primarily within NCT's music.
